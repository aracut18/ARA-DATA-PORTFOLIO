## **Portfolio Projects**
In this section, I list my data analytics projects and briefly describe the technology stack and business impact for each.

### **Beauty E-Commerce Performance Analysis**
**Code:** [Python Analysis (Google Colab)](https://colab.research.google.com/drive/1-kxy4lvQsS7l3kAgLBmz5XozknNBYSai?usp=sharing) | [Raw Dataset](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Mx1t2fH8a3RVZOea8U1IthZSGyobKLTB/view) | [Interactive Tableau Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/hanifah.zahra6513/viz/LunaraPerformanceAnalysis/Dashboard1)

**Goal:** To identify key drivers of low ratings and design targeted operational interventions to increase the average rating from 4.2 to 4.5 within 12 months.

**Description:** The project focused on analyzing LANURA, a Brazilian Beauty & Personal Care e-commerce platform with 12,013 orders over two years. The process involved data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA) on transaction records, analyzing seller/regional performance, and developing an interactive dashboard to recommend SLA enforcement.

**Skills:** Data Cleaning, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Visualization, Business Problem Framing.

**Technology:** Python (Pandas, Numpy), SQL, Tableau Public.

**Results:** The analysis revealed that delivery delays are the strongest predictor of customer dissatisfaction (71% of late deliveries resulted in low ratings, making them 4x more likely to receive poor reviews). Furthermore, it was identified that just 5 sellers account for over 80% of total delivery delays, enabling highly targeted operational interventions.

### **RevoFinance: Expense & Budget Utilization Dashboard**
**Code:** [Project Presentation & Data Details](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1Ldqd_RY2FZXRd524_v9WH5laEFchWrRqSN-KaOcF8w8/edit?slide=id.g3b5c8d4828c_0_1160#slide=id.g3b5c8d4828c_0_1160) | [Interactive Tableau Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/hanifah.zahra6513/viz/DatvizHanifahZahraOCT25/RevoFinanceUserSpendingDashboard?publish=yes)

**Goal:** To help users manage their budgets and make better financial decisions by providing a comprehensive overview of their financial health.

**Description:** This project involved the development of the RevoFinance Expense & Budget Utilization Dashboard. It is designed for app users to monitor spending, track financial patterns, and improve their overall financial condition by comparing actual expenses against planned budgets.

**Skills:** Data Visualization, Dashboard Development, Financial Analysis.

**Technology:** Tableau Public.

**Results:** The dashboard analyzed Rp509M in total expenses across 1,000 transactions. A critical insight showed that the Travel and Subscription categories exceeded their planned budgets (reaching ~110% utilization). Combined, high-risk categories hit 111.1% budget utilization, highlighting a clear need for stricter user spending controls.
